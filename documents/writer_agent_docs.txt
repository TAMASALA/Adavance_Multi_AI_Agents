1. Clear Writing
Good writing explains complex topics in simple language so that readers can understand them easily.

2. Summarization
A good summary captures the main idea of a topic while removing unnecessary details.

3. Professional Tone
Professional writing uses clear language, logical flow, and avoids overly technical jargon unless necessary.

4. Structure
Well-structured writing includes an introduction, explanation, and conclusion.

5. Storytelling
Examples and analogies help readers understand complicated ideas more effectively.
