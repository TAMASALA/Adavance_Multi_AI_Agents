1. Image Prompt Structure
Good image prompts include subject, environment, lighting conditions, and artistic style.

2. Negative Prompts
Negative prompts help diffusion models avoid errors such as blurry images, distortions, or artifacts.

3. Lighting
Lighting styles like cinematic lighting, soft shadows, and volumetric light improve visual quality.

4. Artistic Styles
Popular styles include photorealistic, watercolor, oil painting, anime, and cyberpunk.

5. Camera Settings
Camera descriptions such as 35mm lens, depth of field, and ultra-wide angle improve realism.
