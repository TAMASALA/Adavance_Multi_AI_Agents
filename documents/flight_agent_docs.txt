1. Airline Industry
Airlines provide transportation services between cities and countries using commercial aircraft.

2. Flight Schedules
Flights are defined by airline name, departure time, arrival time, and travel duration.

3. Popular Airlines
Common airlines include Air India, Indigo, Emirates, Qatar Airways, and Singapore Airlines.

4. Direct vs Connecting Flights
Direct flights travel between two cities without stops while connecting flights stop at intermediate airports.

5. Airport Procedures
Passengers must complete check-in, baggage drop, and security screening before boarding flights.
